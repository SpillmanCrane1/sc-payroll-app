Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Spillman & Crane Financial Services, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates an internal accounting tool ("S&C Payroll Tool") that integrates with QuickBooks Online to automate payroll journal entry posting. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information accessed through this application.

1. Information We Access

Our application accesses the following data from your QuickBooks Online account solely for the purpose of posting payroll journal entries:

  • Chart of accounts (to resolve account IDs for journal entry lines)
  • Company information (to verify the correct QuickBooks company is connected)
  • Journal entry creation (write access to post payroll entries)

2. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed through this application is used exclusively for internal accounting operations of Spillman & Crane Financial Services, LLC. Specifically:

  • To create and post payroll journal entries to QuickBooks Online
  • To verify account names match the firm's chart of accounts

We do not use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your QuickBooks data or any other personal or financial information to any third party. This application is an internal tool used solely by authorized personnel of Spillman & Crane Financial Services, LLC.

4. Data Storage

This application does not store QuickBooks data on any external server or database. OAuth credentials (refresh tokens) are stored locally on the firm's internal computers and are accessible only to authorized staff. No financial data is retained beyond what is needed to complete each transaction.

5. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect access credentials and limit access to authorized personnel only. OAuth tokens are stored locally and are never transmitted to third-party services.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your QuickBooks account at any time through the QuickBooks Online app management settings at accounts.intuit.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
