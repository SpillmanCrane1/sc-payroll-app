Frequently Asked Questions

What does this application do?

The S&C Payroll Tool reads a QuickBooks-exported payroll spreadsheet, categorizes wages and deductions according to the firm's chart of accounts, and posts the resulting journal entry directly to QuickBooks Online via the Intuit API.

Who has access to this application?

Access is limited to authorized accounting staff at Spillman & Crane Financial Services, LLC. This is an internal tool and is not available to the general public.

How do I revoke the application's access to QuickBooks?

You can revoke access at any time by logging into your QuickBooks Online account, navigating to Settings → Apps, and disconnecting the S&C Payroll Tool.

What QuickBooks data does this application access?

The application accesses chart of accounts data (read) and journal entries (write) only. No customer, vendor, or transaction history data is accessed or stored.

Who do I contact if something goes wrong?

Please email info@spillmanandcrane.com or call our office during business hours. We typically respond within one business day.
